Projects

adToken Token Launch

On June 26th, 2017, MetaX launched the adToken, the core utility token for the adChain protocol. My contributions toward the adToken Token Launch include:

  • Designing the adToken Logo and all associated branding for marketing purposes
  • Designing the adToken Token Sale site
  • Managing the development of the adToken site

adChain Registry

The adChain Registry is a token curated registry where adToken (ADT) holders curate a list of high-quality, non-fraudulant domains for advertisers to invest their campaign money on. My contributions towards the development of the adChain Registry include:

  • Designing the adChain Registry interface
  • Optimizing and overseeing the development of features for enhanced UX
  • Coordinating development efforts between in-house team and the contracted team
  • Outlining and producing the help center and all assistance materials for seamless user adoption

Ads.txt Plus

Ads.txt Plus is the IAB's ads.txt on the Ethereum blockchain. It upgrades the current edition of ads.txt by allowing enhanced verification and encrypted lists of sellers. My contributions towards the development of Ads.txt Plus include:

  • Mapping the Ads.txt Plus user flow and UX
  • Coordinating the design of the wireframes from a outside design team
  • Managing the development of the DApp
  • Outlining and producing the help center and all assistance materials for seamless user adoption
